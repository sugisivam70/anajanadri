ABOUT  

At ANJANADRI PRECISION, we specialize in delivering high-precision moulding tools, press tools, die-casting, gauges, jigs, and fixtures for the automotive industry. With a focus on quality, innovation, and timely delivery, we provide manufacturing solutions that ensure accuracy, reliability, and exceptional performance in every project.

From product design and development to manufacturing and final delivery, we provide complete end-to-end solutions. Our solutions are built with advanced CNC technology, precision machinery, and strict quality control standards (ISO 9001:2015 certified) to meet automotive and industrial manufacturing needs.
