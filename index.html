ABOUT  

At Genius Tech, we specialize in delivering high-precision CNC machining, press tools, moulds, jigs, and fixtures that power industries across automotive, electrical, and manufacturing sectors. With a focus on quality, innovation, and timely delivery, we provide tooling solutions that ensure accuracy, reliability, and long-term performance.

From progressive tools and strip layouts to welding fixtures, assembly jigs, and high-performance dies, our solutions are built with advanced CNC technology and strict quality control standards to meet global manufacturing needs.
