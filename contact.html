Frequently Asked Questions

Contact us if you need immediate assistance or custom CNC solutions.

01. What CNC services do you offer?

We offer CNC milling, turning, prototyping, batch production, and custom part machining services for various industries.

02. How do I request a quote?

Submit your technical drawings or requirements via our contact form or email, and we’ll provide a detailed quotation.

03. What is the lead time for projects?

Lead times vary based on complexity, but most projects are completed within 7–15 business days.

04. Can you work with custom materials?

Yes, we can work with aluminum, stainless steel, brass, plastics, and more based on your project needs.

05. Do you offer post-processing or finishing?

We provide surface treatments like anodizing, polishing, powder coating, and laser engraving upon request.
