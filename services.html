Machine Shop

Precision machining and toolroom services we undertake

VMC machining, EDM wire cutting, spark erosion, surface grinding, cylindrical grinding, laser engraving, and CMM inspection — all clearly represented here.

Machining and finishing support

Precision VMC machined products for electronic, automotive, aerospace, and other applications in brass, aluminium, and stainless steel.

  • VMC machining: precision part and tool component machining.
  • Wire EDM cutting: fine-profile cutting for high-precision components.
  • Spark erosion: EDM support for cavities and complex tool shapes.
  • Grinding support: surface and cylindrical grinding for close-tolerance finishing.

Shop-floor support infrastructure

A practical setup for both tooling manufacture and outsourced job work execution.

  • VMC units: 3 Yantrang machines for machining capacity.
  • Power press: 100-ton Singhal pneumatic press for punching and forming.
  • Toolroom machines: Oskar sparking, HMT surface grinding, Milltech DRO milling, Oerlikon jig boring, shaping, and H boring.
  • Utilities: Atlas Copco air compressors and bridge crane for safe material handling.
